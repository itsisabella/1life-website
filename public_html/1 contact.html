Andrea Dawson - Company Director and Youth Counsellor

Bach. Health & Social Dev, Dip Counselling and Psychology, Master of Health (Maori Health current study).
Korowai Tupu (Youth Worker Registration) founding member, Ara Taiohi, Member International Counselling Association.

About Andrea

As a current practitioner of youth services, Andrea brings ten years of youth work experience to the Trust.
She specialises in holistic approaches in both therapeutic counselling and mentoring, which includes
adherence to Tikanga Maori approaches. Born and raised in Waitakere, she is still a resident of West Auckland.

Mental Health Crisis Line

Youth Health Hub

The Youth Health Hub is located in West Auckland, North Shore and Rodney,
who provide free health services to young people aged 12-24 living in these areas.
They offer free general Youth Health Clinics by appointment only throughout the week.

Zeal

Zeal's mission is to create spaces and experiences that are accessible to all young people throughout Aotearoa.
Their spaces are places where people can hang, hold events, creative programmes, workshops, online crisis intervention and more.

Youthline

Youthline is an organisation that offers 24/7 Helpline service (phone, text, email and webchat),face to face couselling sessions,
mentoring, programmes in schools and communities This is all free and offered to youth aged 12-24.

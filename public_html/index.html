What We Are About

1LIFE is based in Waitakere offering services to youth (both in school based programmes and 1:1) in our community who experience
mild to moderate mental health challenges by empowering them with resilience skills. The highly effective and proven approach we use is fully interactive
workshops that encompasses life skills, mental wellness coaching and positive self esteem. The framework used promotes
sustainable change over a longer period of time that is greater than other providers can offer. Our framework is underpinned on strength
based approaches, principles of martial arts with the holistic model Te Whare Tapa Wha and Te Ao MƒÅori
being a significant part of the teaching methods applied. Our mantra is promoting sustainable change for our youth,
their whanau and the community. Support our young people by coaching them in vital life skills that uplifts them to be the best that they can.
